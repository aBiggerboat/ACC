About Our City

Sitting snugly between Dallas and Fort Worth, Arlington is home to Six Flags Over Texas, Hurricane Harbor, the International Bowling Museum & Hall of Fame, the Texas Rangers’ Globe Life Park in Arlington and the Dallas Cowboys’ $1 billion AT&T Stadium Complex.
