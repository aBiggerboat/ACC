Shops in Arlington

Arlington Highlands

Along with the more than twenty five places to eat, Arlington Highlands offers over fifty stores to do some shopping, a movie theater, and plenty of places for grown-ups to play.

Gene Allen's Gifts

Gene Allen's is Arlington's #1 Gift Store and is the winner of the Dallas A-List as BEST Gift Shop in 2010, 2012, 2013 & 2014 in the DFW metroplex!

Gracie Lane

Gracie Lane is like a collection of your favorite things — and those you haven’t yet found — all in one pretty package.

The Parks Mall

The Parks Mall is one of the leading shopping destinations in the Metroplex.

Cooper Street Antique Mall

Antiques in Arlington are amazing, and the Antique Mall is the largest antique store in Arlington with over 53,000 square feet of antiques, collectibles, and treasures displayed in over 200 booths and showcases.
