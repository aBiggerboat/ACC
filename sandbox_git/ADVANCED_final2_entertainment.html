Entertainment in Arlington

AT&T Stadium

AT&T Stadium is a retractable roof stadium. It serves as the home of the of the National Football League's Dallas Cowboys.

Globe Life Park

Globe Life Park in Arlington is a state-of-the-art 48,114-seat open-air ballpark and home of Major League Baseball's Texas Rangers.

Six Flags

Six Flags Over Texas is spread over 212 beautiful acres, with 100 acres devoted to themed areas.

Hurricane Harbor

Six Flags Hurricane Harbor has 47 acres of water wonderland to enjoy!

UTA Planetarium

UTA Planetarium is one of the largest planetariums in the State of Texas located in a groundbreaking UTA research facility.
